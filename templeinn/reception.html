Reception

At Temple Inn and Suites we have the perfect wedding reception hall for you. Choose from either the classical themed hall, the modern hall or take your reception outside on our beautiful grounds at each one of our hotels.

With each location, we offer delicious, crowd-pleasing catering, customizable reception halls, and friendly service.




Take advantage of the high end technology and the elegant lighting in each hall to make your special day perfect!




Wedding Package Amenities

We are dedicated to providing convenience, efficiency, and value to your wedding planning experience.

Our standard wedding package includes the following amenities:

  • 280 padded wood, padded distressed wood, or white padded resin folding chairs for the indoor venue
  • 35 five-foot round tables
  • 8 barnwood eight-foot tables
  • Market lights in the garden
  • Private dressing area in the bridal cottage, and each venue in the hotel
  • Lighted and paved garden parking lot for all of our venues
  • Security and parking staff
  • Access to a variety of scenic spots for wedding photography
  • A variety of of decor, including flower arrangements, for table centerpieces
  • White china tableware and flatware
  • Stemmed glassware

You may choose either the on-site caterer or select a caterer of your own.
