Target Audience

Our target audience are for those who live in and near the town of Burke, SD. It is also for those who plan on visiting or find themselves driving through the quaint little town.

We invite anyone of every age that can use a computer or smartphone to explore the website.

The income levels of the area would be from $40,000 and above.
