Visual Hierarchy

Red Headed Hostess

On the Red Headed Hostess's website we can clearly see the path that our hostess wants us to view the content. Her weekly subscription kit is her biggest seller and that's what she wants you to view first.

Rule of Thirds

True Leaf Market

On True Leaf's website, the first thing you see is their banner image. Using the rule of thirds, the banner grabs your attention quickly with its text calling you in. Right after you view the text, your eyes will pan over the image.

Contrast

Humble Bundle

Humble Bundle is a great example of contrast on their website. You can read the colors the text clearly with it being a complete opposite in color to the background and main color of the page.
