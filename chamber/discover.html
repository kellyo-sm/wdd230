Main Street

Welcome to the town of Burke, SD! Here, you will find friendly people and a feeling of family. Take a walk down Main Street and discover our town!

Fun for the Kids

At the Burke Park, we have just built an addition of a swimming pool and a splash pad for the little ones.

During the summer months come by the Burke Park and enjoy the talents offered by the citizens at our Farmer's Market!

Relax

Take a break and go camping near Burke to enjoy the great outdoors. Grab your fishing rods and catch some fish along the Missouri River.

Honoring Our Vets

We appreciate all that our vets have sacrificed for our freedoms. The youth make sure the flags are flown high during Memorial Day weedend.

Sports

Burke offers many sports for the youth and children to enjoy. There's football, basketball, track and field, and of course, swimming!

Population of Burke

Situated in the northern MidWest, the US state of South Dakota is the 17th largest in the Union but has a relatively sparse population by comparison. Like its near neighbor, North Dakota, the state's geographical features do not lend themselves to mass urban settlements, and a lack of choice when it comes to empoloyment has seen an exodus in other parts of the country.
